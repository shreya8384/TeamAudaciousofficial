Posted by Team Audacious on 13th December 2017

Responsible for providing the Power of the Engine to the wheels. Keeping in mind the BAJA concept, we mainly include a CVT for syncing the engine with the gearbox which provides us with infinite gear ratios and also adds to the ergonomics of the vehicle, an important trait for the competition.

For 2017-18 BAJA, our department aimed at achieving maximum efficiency of the engine. This was done with the help of a CVTech CVT along with drive shafts connecting the gearbox to the tyres through the double cardan joints, resulting in better play with smooth and unhindered motion of the wheels. The innovation we have done this time is – Self designed 1speed 2stage reduction gearbox.

This department is being handled by ANKIT KUMAR SINGH.
