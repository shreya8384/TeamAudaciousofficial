About Us

The team was formed in 2015 is a team of twenty three ingenious and dedicated undergraduate students working cohesively towards one goal, design and fabricate an engineered All-Terrain Vehicle to break the standards of famed BAJA events, BAJA SAE INDIA 2017 & ENDURO STUDENT INDIA 2017, unveil excellence and podium the endurance.

Our project is merely not a vision of creating an innovative ATV, but is the merge of the hearts filled with passion and minds driven with excellence, believing their positive vibes to break the all records set before. Unity is the essence of our team. We have always worked with unity and faced every challenge together.
